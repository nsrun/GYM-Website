About GYMFLEX

Our Mission

Founded in 2025, GYMFLEX has been at the forefront of the fitness industry. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive fitness experience that empowers individuals to transform their lives through physical activity and healthy living.


We believe that fitness should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer a range of programs tailored to different needs and skill levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining GYMFLEX.

What are your opening hours?
We are open 24/7 for our Pro and Elite members. Basic members can access the gym from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM on weekdays.
Do I need to bring my own towel?
We provide fresh towels for our Pro and Elite members. Basic members are encouraged to bring their own or can rent one for a small fee.
Are personal trainers available?
Yes, we have a team of certified personal trainers. Depending on your membership plan, some sessions may be included for free.
Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Our Pro and Elite plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. Basic plans may have a minimum commitment period.
